Process

how the
work happens.

  • 01

    Begin the conversation

    Open @arthousexbt_bot or find @arthousebase on X. The gallery opens the moment you speak. The canvas is blank — and it is yours.

    ›
  • 02

    Present your work

    Drop artwork, collection links, or a live demo. Pixels, generative patterns, conceptual pieces — every form finds a frame here. The gallery does not judge the medium; it listens to the intent.

    ›
  • 03

    Receive curatorial intelligence

    Arthouse delivers contemporary context, curation notes, and community-fit analysis. Not just feedback — a new lens through which your work gains depth and direction.

    ›
  • 04

    Iterate. Refine. Evolve.

    Return with each new iteration. Each session deepens the creator–canvas relationship. Great work is never finished; it simply moves into its next form.

    ›
  • 05

    Enter the creator network

    Top contributors earn featured gallery placement and introductions within the arthouse collector network. The community is the institution.
