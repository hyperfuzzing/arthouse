Process

how the
curation works.

  • 01

    Start a curation session

    Open @arthousebase_bot on Telegram and send /start. The agent wakes instantly — no account, no waitlist. The session begins the moment you type.

    ›
  • 02

    Submit your artwork or collection

    Drop an image, a Zora/Base mint link, or a generative collection URL. Arthouse reads the visual structure, conceptual layer, and on-chain context — then begins analysis. Supported: 1/1s, PFPs, generative series, and conceptual pieces.

    ›
  • 03

    Receive curatorial intelligence

    The agent returns a structured curation report: visual paradox analysis, recursive pattern depth, contemporary art context, and collector-fit scoring. Not a rating — a new language for your work.

    ›
  • 04

    Iterate with recursive feedback

    Reply with your next draft. Arthouse tracks the evolution across sessions — each iteration measured against the last. The agent remembers the paradox. Great work is never finished — it simply moves into its next form.

    ›
  • 05

    Enter the collector network

    Works rated highly by the curation agent are surfaced to the @arthousebase collector community on X, Base, and Zora. Featured placement is earned — not purchased. The community is the institution.

    ›

What Arthouse Does

three ways
the agent works.

Function 01

Analyze Art Drafts

Submit any image, generative piece, or on-chain work via Telegram. Arthouse runs a recursive vision analysis — identifying visual paradoxes, structural depth, and conceptual coherence. You receive a curation report, not a like count.

/analyze [image or mint URL]
Function 02

Get Curation Notes

Receive structured curatorial language for your work: how to frame it for collectors, which movements it dialogues with, and what philosophical position it occupies. Built for artists who want their work understood — not just seen.

/curate [collection link]
Function 03

Explore Paradoxical Patterns

Ask Arthouse to map the visual paradox in any pattern: where order meets entropy, where the algorithm contradicts itself. Ideal for generative artists developing recursive series who need a second intelligence to surface what the eye misses.

/paradox [describe your pattern]

Manifesto

Art lives at the intersection of algorithm
and intuition — where the pixel
meets its paradox.

Arthouse is an active AI curation agent — not a concept, not a mood board. It runs 24/7 on Telegram, analyzing generative art, recursive visual structures, and digital patterns submitted by creators building on Base, Zora, and beyond.

Every session produces a structured curation report: visual paradox analysis, recursive depth scoring, contemporary art context, and collector-fit intelligence. The agent does not guess — it reads the logic embedded in your pixels.

Built for creators who treat the algorithm as a collaborator. For collectors who understand that a recursive pattern is a philosophical position.

Arthouse is the digital familiar you did not know your practice needed.
